The Best Movies According to George

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Finian's Rainbow

This is one of my favorite musicals! I love the crossover between life in the American South and Irish folklore.

The Music Man

This is another one of my favorite musicals! It has a wonderful story, an awesome cast, and fun songs.

Terrifier 2

This is one of my favorite horror films! I love the 80s flair, the characters, and the suspenseful atmosphere.
